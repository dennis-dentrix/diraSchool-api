# DiraSchool — School Presentation & Sales Guide

> A practical guide for pitching DiraSchool to Kenyan school administrators, bursars, and principals.

---

## 1. The One-Sentence Pitch

> **"DiraSchool replaces your exercise books, Excel files, and WhatsApp groups with one simple system — and parents pay fees directly through M-Pesa."**

Use this line first. It anchors everything that follows.

## 3. Core Value Propositions (Memorise These)

### 3.1 M-Pesa Fee Collection — Your #1 Differentiator
- Parents send money to the school paybill; the system **automatically matches the payment** to the student.
- The bursar gets a **real-time notification** on the dashboard the moment payment arrives.
- A **PDF receipt** is generated instantly and can be sent by SMS or printed.
- **Zero manual reconciliation.** No more chasing parents on WhatsApp.

> *"Imagine knowing exactly which students have paid — before morning assembly."*

### 3.2 CBC-Ready Report Cards
- Built specifically for Kenya's CBC curriculum grading (RL, AP, EE, ME thresholds).
- Generate report cards for an entire class with one click.
- PDFs are stored in the cloud — print anytime, never lost.

### 3.3 Everything in One Place
- Student records, attendance, exams, results, timetables, library, transport, payroll — one login.
- Role-based access: teachers see only their classes; bursars see only fees; principal sees everything.
- Works on any device with a browser — no app installation needed.

### 3.4 Real-Time Attendance
- Mark attendance per class per day.
- Dashboard shows live attendance rate across the school.
- Absence reports generated automatically.

### 3.5 SMS & Email Alerts to Parents
- Automatic SMS when a student is absent.
- Fee payment confirmation sent to parent's phone.
- Exam results notifications.
- Powered by Celcom Africa — reliable Kenyan SMS gateway.

### 3.6 Cloud-Based & Secure
- Data stored securely; no risk of losing records if a laptop is stolen or crashes.
- Automatic backups. Access from the office, home, or phone.
- Multi-school capable — ideal for school groups and chains.

## 4. The Demo Flow (15-Minute School Visit)

Run this sequence on your laptop or tablet. Have test data pre-loaded.

**Minute 0–2 — Hook**
> Open the dashboard. Show the summary cards: students enrolled, fees collected today, attendance rate.
> Say: *"This is what your bursar and principal would see every morning."*

**Minute 2–5 — M-Pesa Payment**
> Show the Fees section. Show a list of students with paid/unpaid status.
> Show a payment record with the M-Pesa transaction ID and the auto-generated receipt PDF.
> Say: *"When a parent pays via M-Pesa, this appears automatically. No one typed it in."*

**Minute 5–8 — Student & Attendance**
> Open a student profile — photo, guardian contacts, class, fee balance.
> Navigate to Attendance — mark a class present/absent. Show the report.
> Say: *"Teachers mark attendance in 2 minutes. You get a report instantly."*

**Minute 8–11 — Report Cards**
> Go to Report Cards. Show a generated CBC report card PDF.
> Say: *"One click generates report cards for the whole class. No more manual typing."*

**Minute 11–13 — Timetable & Exams**
> Show the timetable builder — classes, subjects, teachers assigned.
> Show the exam results entry page.

**Minute 13–15 — Pricing & Next Steps**
> Show the Pricing page.
> Say: *"We charge a flat monthly fee per school — no per-transaction charges on M-Pesa, no hidden fees."*
> Hand them a leave-behind (see Section 7).

## 5. Handling Common Objections

| Objection | Your Response |
|-----------|---------------|
| *"We already use Excel."* | "Excel doesn't send SMS to parents, generate CBC report cards, or receive M-Pesa payments. DiraSchool does all three — and it's always backed up." |
| *"Our teachers aren't tech-savvy."* | "If they can use WhatsApp, they can use DiraSchool. We also provide onboarding training." |
| *"We don't have reliable internet."* | "The system works on any data connection, including mobile data. Teachers can mark attendance on a phone." |
| *"It's too expensive."* | "What does one lost fee payment cost you? One month of manual receipt books? DiraSchool pays for itself in the first week of term." |
| *"Who hosts our data? Is it safe?"* | "Data is hosted on DigitalOcean servers with daily backups. It's the same infrastructure used by global SaaS companies." |
| *"What if you shut down?"* | "We can export all your data to Excel or CSV at any time. Your data is always yours." |
| *"Can it handle our school size?"* | "The system is built to scale — from 50 to 5,000 students. Pricing adjusts per student count." |
| *"Do you support NEMIS?"* | "We store all the fields required for NEMIS reporting. Export is available. Full NEMIS sync is on our roadmap." |

## 6. Pricing Conversation Guide

**Current model:**
- Base fee: **KES 7,500/month**
- Per student: **KES 50/student/month**
- Example: School of 300 students → KES 7,500 + (300 × 50) = **KES 22,500/month**

**How to frame it:**
> *"For a school of 300 students that's about KES 750 per student per year — less than the cost of a single exercise book per child. And you're getting fee collection, report cards, attendance, SMS alerts, and more."*

**Negotiation levers you can offer:**
- First month free (trial term)
- Annual payment discount (2 months free)
- Free onboarding and staff training
- Free data migration from their current system

## 7. Leave-Behind (What to Send/Print After the Visit)

Send a follow-up email within 2 hours containing:
1. A one-page PDF summary of DiraSchool features (tailor to their pain points)
2. A demo video link (record a Loom of the 15-minute demo above)
3. The pricing breakdown for their specific student count
4. Your WhatsApp number for quick questions
5. A link to book a follow-up call/visit

**Subject line for the email:**
> *"DiraSchool Demo for [School Name] — Summary & Next Steps"*

## 8. Questions to Ask During the Visit (Discovery)

Use these to qualify the school and tailor your pitch:

1. *"How do you currently track which parents have paid fees?"*
2. *"Do you use M-Pesa Paybill or Till for fee collection?"*
3. *"How long does it take your office to generate report cards at end of term?"*
4. *"How do you communicate with parents when a student is absent?"*
5. *"How many students do you have currently?"*
6. *"Do you have a school group — more than one branch?"* ← upsell opportunity
7. *"Who makes the decision on adopting new software?"* ← identify the real buyer
